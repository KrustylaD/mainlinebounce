WE HAD A DREAM !

CARLINE AND JONATHAN GARCIA ARE THE PROUD OWNERS OF MAIN LINE BOUNCE AND PARENTS OF FIVE CHILDREN. WITH THAT MANY KIDDOES, WE'RE ALWAYS PLANNING BIRTHDAY PARTIES. WE'VE CELEBRATED AT ARCADES, TRAMPOLINE PARKS, SCHOOLS, AND INDOOR TODDLER PLAY CENTERS — BUT HOME WAS ALWAYS OUR FAVORITE.

HOSTING AT HOME MEANT SHARING OUR HUMBLE SPACE WITH A BUNCH OF STRANGERS, AND IT GAVE US THE CHANCE TO MEET OTHER PARENTS WHO LATER BECAME FRIENDS. THROUGH OUR SPECIAL-NEEDS PARENTING JOURNEY WE MET WONDERFUL PARENTS AND EDUCATORS WE'RE STILL IN TOUCH WITH. THOSE CONNECTIONS SHOWED US HOW IMPORTANT COMMUNITY IS TO A HAPPY LIFE.

CELEBRATIONS BRING PEOPLE TOGETHER, AND WE WANTED TO BE A PART OF THAT. THAT'S HOW MAINLINE BOUNCE WAS BORN. 😊
